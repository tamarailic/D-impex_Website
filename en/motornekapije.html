Kits and motors for automatic gates

Motorized gates are opened and closed by remote control control or mobile phone and make up your home or business premises safer and more comfortable. Our offer includes engines for monoplanes, motors for double doors and motors for sliding gates as well as automatic ones parking ramps.

We offer the installation of motors for the automatic opening and closing of gates, namely: single-wing (opening to the side by 90 degrees); two-winged (simultaneous opening of both wings or only one, if desired) i sliding gates (opening by moving the entire gate to the side). We install motors for opening and closing roller garage doors, segmental and statue doors, raising and lowering parking ramps using remote.

We use engines from the Italian manufacturer Proteco in three variants, u depending on the size and weight of the gate. We have IR barriers to protection of the direction of movement of the gate and a strobe lamp for signaling the operation of the gate. We enable gate management, opening, and closing, using mobile phone applications via WiFi or GSM systems. As per if desired, we install an additional lock with a key for mechanical opening in case of loss of remote control.
