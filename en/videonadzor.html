Familiarize yourself with camera possibilities

Video surveillance is a system consisting of one or more surveillance systems cameras connected to a central device that performs signal processing. The system can be monitored via the Internet and mobile phones. The applications we use to support both Android and iPhone phone. The installation of video surveillance increases the safety and security of people and property at risk. Police use video surveillance works detect criminal activities and prevention unauthorized entry into buildings (anti-burglary effect) and monitoring of movement around protected areas and in protected premises.

IP video security is a lossless, capable transmission system transmission of very high resolutions, with exceptional freedom and flexibility when planning cable installation. If you own the facility's computer network, it can be used for this type of camera, which reduces installation costs to a minimum. Also, this system allows image capture on multiple servers, as well as integration with other systems, such as access control, alarm systems, etc.

HD video security provides significantly higher resolution and image quality, lossless compared to an analog system. Also, a digital signal is transmitted through a standard coaxial cable that is also used for analog video security, so replacing the whole thing is easily feasible or part of the analog camera system, without the need to change the existing one installations. HD video security is an ideal solution for drastic image improvement of the old video security system, without major works and installation costs.

Analog video security is the cheapest option, but the maximum the resolution you can achieve with this type of camera is 960 pixels. That's why a large number of people put image quality first Instead, he decides on HD or IP cameras, which are not much more expensive provide higher resolution.
