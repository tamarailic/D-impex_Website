Alarm Systems for home and business

Alarm systems are designed to detect unauthorized intrusion residential, business, or any other protected area or facility. They are installed both for business and private purposes. Various alarms telephone (IP, GSM, GPRS, 3G, and 4G), light and sound signals alert the guards or owners of some property and make a report.

There are several types of these systems. The first type is represented by systems that have the purpose of protecting against unauthorized intrusion, and next to them, there are also combined systems that protect against intrusion and fire. They consist of several different types of sensors, alarm centers with backup power supplies, sirens, and warning devices. Depending on the type: sensors detect movement, sound (glass breakage), vibrations, microwaves, smoke, temperature increase, water, and gas. Sensors can be connected to the alarm center by wired or wireless connection depending on the type of system.
